LAURA FISHER

Laura Fisher is a New Orleans-based composer, vocalist, pianist, arranger, and producer. Her genres of influence and interest include alternative pop, art rock, electronica, shoegaze, and neo-classical. Fisher has competed, collaborated, and performed all over: from DIY venues in Savannah, GA and Huntsville, AL to caves off the coast of Portland, ME; from a forest in southern NJ to a famous ski destination in Niseko, Japan; from the Birmingham Museum of Art to Lincoln Center in New York City.

Her current projects include a series of solo releases ranging from ambient neoclassical & electronica, to alternative pop; as well as being a vocalist, songwriter, and keyboardist for the art rock/math pop band Matron and the front woman and co-writer for former dreamscape grunge/alternative rock band Tranche.
